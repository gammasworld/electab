History

Electab Corporation has been in operation since 2004 manufacturing electronic assemblies, harnesses and electromechanical assemblies. The business is owned and operated by an American Corporation. We know that controlled growth is the only way we can achieve our goals. We know the value of honest commitments, not just making promises to get business. We will not accept business or make commitments we can not achieve.

Our work force is managed by a group of key employees who have many years experience in electronic and electromechanical manufacturing for large manufacturing companies in Mexico Victor J. Gonzalez, President and partner of Electab Corporation, manages the complete operation and has over 35 years experience in engineering and manufacturing. We are currently manufacturing traffic related controls, detectors, message boards, lamps control panels harnesses etc. Large important companies all of them located in the USA.
