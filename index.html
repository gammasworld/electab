Our Services:

Shelter Operation

International Assemblers will provide excellent service, the factory space, the required personnel, the labor, and the infrastructure to operate your maquiladora or manufacturing facility in Mexico. Also included is customs service, bringing your technology, material and necessary equipment across the U.S./Mexico border, the warehouse on the U.S. side and the daily guidance to succeed in Mexico.

Contract Manufacturing

We have a piece-rate assembly service providing labor-value-added assembly in a subcontract format. International Assemblers, is the subcontractor to manufacture your products in Mexico. The customer provides the material and the equipment and we provide the rest.
